The Problem

There is little to no effort locally and across Africa to educate the public about congenital disorders. As a result, some families and individuals fall short to stereotypes and beliefs about some of these conditions. CURE Hospital Uganda, a donor funded hospital in Uganda who specialize in neurosurgery and specifically hydrocephalus and spina bifida have some really shocking findings. In some villages in Uganda, families KILL their own children! Children who are born with Hydrocephalus and Spina Bifida are usually considered cursed or affected by witchcraft. There are cases where these children are left to die locked up in houses, eventually they succumb to the effects of non treatment. These kids are secretly buried and forgotten about. In extreme cases, the women accompanied by their kin drown the kids in nearby rivers.

Creating awareness

Knowledge is power. By making access to information readily available we will be able to educate the public on the causes, treatments plans, where to access treatment, expectations to prepare them psychologically for any future challenges and much more.
